Chortle Portal - Saturday March 4th, 2017

Chortle Portal was a fun indie show in the basement of an apartment building. There is no way it was up to fire code, but it was a lot of fun nonetheless. The crowds at this show were definitely the target audience for my style of comedy. The rest of the week is just downhill from here.

Nasty's Open Mic - Saturday March 4th, 2017

Did this open mic after Chortle Portal. It was in a fun little dive bar. I went up second to last, and I was afraid no one would be paying attention, but my set went well. PBRs were too expensive though.
